## Observations (Not Bugs)

1. **Import AI cleanup fallback:** The receipt parser's AI cleanup step failed silently and fell back to rule-based parsing, which produced correct results. The ambiguity message `"AI cleanup failed, using rule-based results"` is surfaced to the user, which is the right behavior. The fallback is working as designed.

2. **Enrichment table lacks foreign key:** `remi_enrichments.wineId` is not a foreign key to `wines.id`. If a wine is deleted, its enrichment row persists as orphaned data. This is harmless (the enrich endpoint checks wine existence before returning) but could accumulate stale rows over time. Low priority cleanup candidate.

3. **Email drafting is conversational, not templated:** When asked to draft an email, Remi first asked clarifying questions, then provided a wine list rather than a formatted email. This is a reasonable AI behavior choice — it could be enhanced to produce actual email prose, but it's not a bug.
